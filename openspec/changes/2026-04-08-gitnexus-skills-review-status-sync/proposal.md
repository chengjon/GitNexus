## Why

The historical `docs/gitnexus-skills-review.md` report still reads like a
current status board for the seven GitNexus skills, even though several of its
original drift findings have already been addressed by later convergence work.

That leaves a historical review document easy to misread as a current backlog.

## What Changes

- Add status-sync framing to `docs/gitnexus-skills-review.md`
- Add a current follow-up snapshot that points readers to later convergence
  records and current skill-doc reality
- Preserve the original 2026-03-26 review content as historical context

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `gitnexus-skills-review-status-sync`: Keep the historical skills-review
  report readable as a baseline without misrepresenting current skill-doc
  status.

### Modified Capabilities

- None.
